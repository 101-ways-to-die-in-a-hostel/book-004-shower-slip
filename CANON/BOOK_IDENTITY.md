## The Death (One Sentence)
A hungover party backpacker slips on the moldy, tile-less shower floor of a legendary party hostel, cracking his skull on the exposed pipe and bleeding out while everyone assumes he's just sleeping off another bender.

## Tagline
"He came to Prague to get wasted. The shower finished the job."

## Themes
1. The culture of excess in backpacker party hostels
2. How normalized dysfunction becomes invisible danger
3. The dark side of "you only live once" when you only die once too

## Tone Balance

## Target Reader
Anyone who's nursed a hangover in a hostel shower, travelers who've partied too hard in Prague or Budapest, and people who've ignored their bodies screaming to slow down.
